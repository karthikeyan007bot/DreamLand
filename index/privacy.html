Privacy Policy for Fanta Verse

Last updated : 6/5/2023

At Fanta Verse, we take your privacy as seriously as a dragon guards its treasure. Please read our privacy policy to understand how we collect, use, and protect your information, so you can rest easy while you embark on your imaginative adventures.

Information We Collect

When you register on Fanta Verse, we collect your name, email address, and birthdate. We promise we won't ask for your favorite flavor of ice cream or your secret recipe for unicorn pancakes.

Collection Methods

We gather this information during the registration process, whether you sign up directly or choose the swift path of Google sign-in. You can also update your profile to keep it as shiny and magical as a rainbow.

Use of Information

We use your information to sprinkle a bit of personalized magic onto your Fanta Verse experience. From tailoring recommendations to customizing your user interface, we want to ensure your dreams unfold exactly the way you imagine them.

Data Security

Rest assured, we guard your information with the tenacity of a knight protecting their kingdom. We rely on the trusty armor of MongoDB as our storage system, implementing industry-standard security measures to keep your data safe. We promise not to share your information with wandering wizards or mischievous elves.

Updates to Privacy Policy

Our privacy policy may undergo occasional transformations, just like a caterpillar turning into a butterfly. We'll let you know when these changes occur by displaying the "last updated" date on top of the privacy policy page. So, keep an eye out for any surprises, magical or otherwise!

Contact Information

If you have any questions, concerns, or just want to share your most fantastic dreams with us, you can reach us by email at fantaverse@gmail.com. Our friendly team of dream-weavers is always ready to assist you.
